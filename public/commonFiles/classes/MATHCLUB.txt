Xilin Math Circle

Members: 9th - 12th graders

Purposes:

To provide an environment in which members with shared passion and interest for mathematics can learn and discuss with each other
To provide opportunities for members to learn more about mathematics

Goals:

To help club members qualify and compete in math competitions such as AMC/AIME
To inform club members of opportunities that will enrich their mathematics
To strengthen club members’ overall knowledge and understanding of mathematics
Activities:
Lessons to help members learn new topics about mathematics and strengthen existing knowledge
Problem solving sessions to allow members to apply the skills they have learned
Practice and preparation for competitive math
